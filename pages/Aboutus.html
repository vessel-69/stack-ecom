About STACK

Five students who thought building a fake e-commerce site for a school project was a W idea — and honestly it was

The Story

Where it started

STACK was born out of a web development workshop in 2023 — real ones know the grind of building something from scratch with zero clue what you're doing

The goal was simple: build a two-page e-commerce website using only HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no copying off the internet, no cap

We picked tech and gaming gear because we lowkey spend all our money on that stuff anyway — so at least the product descriptions would be accurate

What STACK is

STACK is a concept e-commerce store built as a school project by Group B — it features gaming gear, PC components, fitness equipment, and whatever else we thought was cool at the time

The name "STACK" comes from the idea of stacking your setup — building something piece by piece until it goes absolutely crazy

Inspired by Flipkart, Daraz, and RemoveAI — we tried to build something that felt real even if the prices say "FREE" on everything
